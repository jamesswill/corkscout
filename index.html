Wines the best sommeliers pour by the glass — decoded into bottles you can buy.

A wine earns a by-the-glass slot at a serious restaurant because someone with a sommelier's palate vouched for it — and the house makes its margin on the pour. So the same bottle, bought retail, is often a quietly excellent value. Pick a wine type, narrow by neighborhood or restaurant, and we'll point you to the bottle and where to buy it.
